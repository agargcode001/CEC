Activities > Freshers

Summary

Team CEC organized the civil department's official freshers for the batch 2018-22 on 23 August 2018. The preparations were started in the first week of August. By organizing freshers we served the purpose to increase the junior-senior interaction. The second-year students guided the first-year students to prepare their performances. In this event not only CEC members were involved but many other enthusiastic second-year students showed up to help. Professors of the Civil department were invited to this event. We also organized Mr. & Ms. Fresher's competition in which the freshmen participated enthusiastically. We provided snacks for that evening. The OP Jain auditorium was decorated properly and gifts were presented to the winners of Mr. & Ms. Fresher competition.

First year batch engaged themselves in various performances namely -

  1. Anchoring
  2. Comedy skit
  3. Group Dance
  4. Solo Dance
  5. Group song
  6. Serious skit
  7. Solo singing
  8. Poetry
A freshers video was made by our present third year which covered the off-stage practices,behind the scenes, some questioning to freshmen about their experience in college and many more. This video crossed 13k views on youtube.
