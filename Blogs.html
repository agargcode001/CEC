Guggenheim Museum Bilbao
27th March 2020
An absolute icon of contemporary architecture of the nineties, the building of Guggenheim Museum Bilbao, located in Spain, features distorted and oblique volumes and sets an outlandish example of sculptural architecture. It sums up in itself merits and ambiguities of the new architectural languages in the years between the end of the twentieth century and the beginning of the third Millennium.
